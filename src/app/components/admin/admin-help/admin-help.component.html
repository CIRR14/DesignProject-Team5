    Employees

  • What does the employee tab have?

    Employees tab is where you can manage your employees. You can assign roles of either an employee or an admin. This is also the place to verify an employee. Another feature of the Employees tab is to change rate of pay.

  • How do I add an employee?

    An Employee can be added by first providing him/her the url of the app which is https://gd-management-system.firebaseapp.com/login.

    Have the employee sign in with their gmail account

    It will display that it is users' first time logging in and needs access

    you would then need to follow the steps below to approve the employee

    Step 1) click on the Employees tab

    Step 2) Recent employees that need approval will be displayed with orange color with "Not Approved" written

    Roles are listed as:
    ADMIN = Blue
    EMPLOYEE = Green
    NOT APPROVED = Orange

    The selected employee can be edited in these steps:

    Step 1) Enter/edit employee name
    Step 2) Enter employee ID
    Step 3) Enter hourly rate
    Step 4) Role to decide whether the employee is admin or employee
    **If there are any changes made, click "UPDATE EMPLOYEE" button to update information**
    **If there are no changes, please click "CANCEL"**

  • How do I delete an employee?

    An Employee can be deleted by first clicking on the employee tab and by following the steps below:

    Step 1) Click on the employee you would like to delete

    Step 2) press the delete button located on the top right

    You will get a warning saying "Are Your Sure You Want to Delete This Employee?"

    Click "DELETE"


    In case if you deleted an employee accidently, you will need to request an employee to login in with their gmail account again in this following provided URL: https://gd-management-system.firebaseapp.com/login

    Check Availabilities

  • How do I check availabilities of my employees?

    You can check employees availability from the Availabilities tab.

    1. "TODAY": brings you back to the current date. e.g.If you go to a future date or a past date, then "TODAY" enables you to quickly go back to today's date.

    2. "DAY": shows you the specific date that you selected

    3. "WEEK": shows you the specific week you selected from Sunday to Saturday

    4. "WORK WEEK": shows you the specific work week you selected from Monday to Friday

    4. "MONTH": shows you a specific month you selected

    5. "AGENDA" : shows you who are available in a list format with the dates

    Active Jobs

  • 1. How do I create a job?

    The Jobs tab has all the jobs that are active.

    You can also add a new job by clicking add job at the top right corner

    This is add job page:

    Step 1) Select/Enter when job was created
    **cannot be changed later

    Step 2) Enter client's name

    Step 3) Enter client's address
    **cannot be changed later

    Step 4) Enter Job ID (Generates automatically with all house number and first three letters of the street)
    **cannot be changed later

    Step 5) Enter job description

    Step 6) After filling everything "Submit" will appear. Simply click "Submit" button

    You can also click "Cancel" to go back

  • 2. How do I edit a job?

    A Job can be edited by clicking on a specific job that is already created. You will not be able to edit the address and the date job was created

    If you need to make adjustments, please follow these steps:

    Step 1) Edit client name

    Step 2) Edit jobhours

    Step 3) Edit status to active job or inactive job
    **once you select to inactive, the job will move into inactive job table

    Step 4) Edit description

    Step 5) Once you made changes, please click "UPDATE JOB"
