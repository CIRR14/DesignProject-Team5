Frequently Asked Questions

    Dashboard

  • What's in the Dashboard?

    The Dashboard displays all current employees, their rate of pay, hours worked and their total pay for the hours worked. After everything is verified, you can also export it as a PDF by clicking Export on the top right

    Employees

  • What does the employee tab have?

    Employees tab is where you can manage your employees. You can assign roles of either an emploee or an admin. This is also the place to verify an employee. Another feature of the Employees tab is to change rate of pay.

    Check Availabilities

  • How do I check availabilities of my employees?

    You can check employees availability from the Availabilities tab.

    Jobs

  • How do I create a job?

    The Jobs tab has all the created jobs. You can also add a new job by clicking add job at the top right corner

    Export

  • How do I export document after everything is verified?

    You can use the export function located in the Dashboard.
