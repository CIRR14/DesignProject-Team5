Frequently Asked Questions(FAQS)

    ClockIn/Out

  • How do I Clock In?

    You Can Clock In from the Clock Hours tab located on the left hand side

    After pressing the Clock Hours tab

    Step 1) select a Job

    Step 2) Press the Clock In button

    How do I clock out?

    Step 3) You Can Clock Out from the Clock Hours tab located on the left hand side and then by pressing Clock Out.

  • How do I submit my hours if i forget to clock in?

    You can submit hours worked by checking the "forgot to clock in?" button in the Clock Hours tab underneath the clock in and out.

    Step 1) press the "forgot to clock in? check this box to add hours" button

    step 2) select the date that you forgot to clock in.
    ** You can only select the date from the past or current day but not future dates **

    step 3) Select the job related to missed clock-in

    Step 4) Enter total hours worked on that job

    step 5) Press Submit

    Availability

  • How do I submit my availabilities?

    You can submit availabilities by clicking on Availability tab located on the left side below your profile

    step 1) select the dates you are available

    Step 2) click submit

  • How do I change my availability after its already submitted?

    You can change availability by clicking on Availability tab

    Step 1) Unselect the date(s) that you previously submitted

    ** Make sure that the dates you are unavailable is not red **

    Step 2) Press Submit

    Sign In/Out of App

  • How do I sign in and out?

    You can sign in to the app by going to the following URL: https://gd-management-system.firebaseapp.com/login

    You can then sign in using your gmail account. Your administrator will need to approve you

    You can sign out of your account by clicking the logout button
