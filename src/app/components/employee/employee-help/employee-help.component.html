ClockIn/Out

  • How do I Clock In and Out?

    You Can Clock In from the Clock Hours tab by pressing the Clock In button.

    You Can Clock Out from the Clock Hours tab by pressing the Clock out button.

    Availability

  • How do I submit my availabilities?

    You can submit availabilities by clicking on Availability tab. Just select the dates you are available to work and click submit.

  • How do I change my availabilities after its submitted?

    You can change availabilities by clicking on Availability tab. Just unselect the dates you already submitted and select the ones available to work and click submit.

    The Date automatically turns red once its selected and stays red when submitted.

    Sign In/Out of App

  • How do I sign in and out?

    You can sign in to the app using your gmail account.

    You can signout of your account by clicking the logout button
